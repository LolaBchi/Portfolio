Who I am

Coming from a coastal town in France, I've always been passionate about Life Sciences. At 18 years old, I decided to move to Paris for my studies, where I pursued a bachelor's degree in Cell Biology and Physiology, followed by a research master's in Pathophysiology, specializing in Nutrition and Metabolism. Driven by my innate curiosity and a desire for a pluridisciplinary field of research, I found my first job as a biology engineer in an academic laboratory focused on Respiratory Neurophysiology, where I worked for three years.

In mid-2021, amid the COVID-19 pandemic and facing limited exciting opportunities in the academic sector, I decided to expand my knowledge with a Master of Science in Biotechnology.
